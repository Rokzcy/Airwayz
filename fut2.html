About FUT Minna

Established in 1983, the Federal University of Technology, Minna has been at the forefront of technological innovation and academic excellence in Nigeria.

With over 20,000 students across two campuses (Gidan Kwano and Bosso), we provide world-class education and cutting-edge research opportunities in science and technology.
